Call for papers

Modern computer systems are increasing in scale, spread, and complexity. The specifications of such systems cannot be made complete due to the fact that the modification of systems has to be made continuously to meet users' ever-changing requirements, hardware and communication systems changes, to avoid attacks, and so forth. The modification of the systems usually has to be performed while the services are being provided. This workshop aims to foster discussions about new concepts, processes, architectures and tools for Open Systems Dependability. Ongoing researches are welcome to join this workshop.
